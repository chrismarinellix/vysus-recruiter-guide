Technical Experience We'll Discuss

Grid Connection Studies

We'd like to hear about your experience with connection studies, including:

  • R1 (model-based) and R2 (commissioning) studies
  • Grid code compliance demonstration
  • Working with network operators (AEMO, TNSPs)

Simulation Tools

Please be prepared to discuss your proficiency with power system simulation software:

  • PSCAD for EMT studies
  • PSS/E or DIgSILENT for RMS/steady-state analysis
  • Any scripting or automation experience (Python, etc.)

Inverter-Based Resources

Understanding of modern renewable generation is important:

  • Grid-Following vs Grid-Forming inverter concepts
  • System strength and SCR (Short Circuit Ratio)
  • Plant Primary Controller (PPC) functionality

Project Experience

We're interested in hearing about specific projects you've contributed to:

  • Project scale (MW capacity)
  • Your specific role and responsibilities
  • Challenges you overcame
